Privacy Policy

This Policy explains how the 3Parusa project ("we") processes the personal data of visitors to https://3parusa.com.

1. What data we collect

  • Form data: name, phone number, email address and chosen destination — when you submit a request.
  • Anonymous data: visit information (pages, referral source, device) via the Yandex.Metrica analytics service using cookies.

2. Purposes of processing

To contact you about your request, to arrange and organise yacht tours, and to improve the website and analyse traffic.

3. Sharing with third parties

Form data is processed by Formspree and delivered to our email. Web analytics is provided by Yandex.Metrica. We do not sell or share your data with any other third parties.

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on your consent, which you give by submitting a form and continuing to use the site.

5. Storage and deletion

We keep data only as long as needed to contact you and organise a tour. You may request changes or deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Cookies

The site uses cookies for analytics (Yandex.Metrica). You can disable cookies in your browser settings — this will not affect the core functions of the site.
